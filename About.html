ABOUT US



Established in 2020, Educate Girl's is a non-profit that focusses on mobilising communities for girl's education in India’s rural and educationally backward areas. Strongly aligned with the ‘Right to Education Act’.

Educate Girls is committed to the Government’s vision to improve access to primary education for children, especially young girls.
